FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common support questions from our customers.

What training is included when I buy a system?
+
We provide full system usage training when we install your system. Our technician walks your staff through every feature — POS, inventory, reports, and user management. We do not provide general business training, only system usage training.
What do I do if my system stops working?
+
Contact us immediately via WhatsApp (+250 780 000 000) or phone. Our team will troubleshoot remotely first, and if necessary, schedule an on-site visit within 24–48 hours. Critical issues are prioritized for same-day response.
Can I get support for new staff who need training?
+
Yes. Contact us and we'll schedule a refresher training session. Basic remote guidance is free. On-site refresher sessions may incur a small fee depending on your plan.
How often is the system updated?
+
We release updates regularly — typically every 1–2 months. Updates include bug fixes, security patches, and new features. All updates are applied automatically and are always free.
Is my business data safe if my computer crashes?
+
Yes. Your data is automatically backed up every day to a secure server. If your hardware fails, we restore your complete business data with minimal downtime. Included in all subscription plans.
Do you provide support on weekends?
+
WhatsApp support is available 7 days a week for urgent issues. Phone support runs Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM. For emergencies outside these hours, WhatsApp us and we'll respond as quickly as possible.
Can I get support in Kinyarwanda or French?
+
Yes. Our support team communicates in Kinyarwanda, French, and English. Reach us in whichever language is most comfortable for you.
