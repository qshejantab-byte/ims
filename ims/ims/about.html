Who We Are

Integrated Management Solutions

IMS Company is a Kigali-based technology company dedicated to building smart, affordable business management systems for small and medium enterprises across Rwanda and Africa.

We specialize in Point-of-Sale (POS), inventory management, and operations software — tailored to the realities of Rwandan businesses with local payment methods and local customer service.
