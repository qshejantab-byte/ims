Pricing FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

What does the installation fee cover?
+
The 50,000 RWF covers complete setup — hardware configuration, software installation, database setup, product loading, and full staff training. Paid once only.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
+
Yes. You can upgrade at any time by contacting our team. The price difference is prorated for the remaining months in your billing year.
How do I pay my annual subscription?
+
Via MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Bank Transfer, Payment Link, or Cash. Payment is made once per year. Our team sends a renewal reminder 30 days before your annual renewal date.
Are there any hidden fees?
+
No. The installation fee and annual subscription are all you pay. No setup charges, no update fees, no hidden costs.
What if I want to cancel?
+
Cancel any time by contacting us. No cancellation fees. Your system stays active until end of your current billing period.
